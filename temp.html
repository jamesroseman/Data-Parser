Free Alumni Career Teleseminar: Networking for Success

Sponsored by:

Tufts Alumni Career Services

When: Thursday, April 07, 2011, 12:00 pm (iCal)

Description

Leslie Warner, Tufts Alumni Career Services, will lead a one-hour interactive tele-seminar on successful networking strategies used by top executives and networking masters. Whether your goals include making your next career move, increasing your visibility and effectiveness in your current company, or growing your business, this workshop will provide tactical networking skills critical to your success.
